Pasta

Description

This is fabilious Spaghetti pasta that will make you crave for it.

It has fresh aged parmesan cheese right on top of it.

After first bite,you couldn't help but finish the ENTIRE plate and ask more of it!

Ingredients

  • Barilla Spaghetti from Grocery Store
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 3/4 Tomatos
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 Spoontable salt

Steps

  1. Unpack the pasta,put into boiling water.
  2. Start to preparing pasta sauce while the pasta boiling on the pan.
  3. After 10 minutes,check the softness of the pasta,drain it to the sink.
  4. Pour the Pasta sauce on top of the Spaghetti,sprinkle the delicious parmesan cheese as a final step.
  5. It's ready to eat,WHO'S HUNGRY??!
